ONWARD

2020   Animation, Adventure, Comedy

In a suburban fantasy world, two teenage elf brothers, lan and
Barley Lightfoot, go on a journey to discover if there is still a little
magic left out there in order to spend one last day with their father,
who died when they were too young to remember him. Like any
good quest, their journey is filled with magic spells, cryptic maps,-
impossible obstacles and unimaginable discoveries. Perilous curses
aside, this one magical day.could mean more than any of them ever
dreamed.
